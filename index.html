I am a PhD student in Machine Learning & Systems at Virginia Tech SNAIL Lab under Dr. Bo Ji's advising. My research focuses on optimizing the training and inference of multimodal LLMs, specializing in techniques like efficient self-attention, token dropping, layer skipping, sparsity, and quantization. I have prior experience in the research and development of AI-driven recommendation systems at IQVIA. I am passionate about advancing the field of AI, particularly in the areas of large language models and machine learning systems.
