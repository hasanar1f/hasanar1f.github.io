I'm a PhD student in Machine Learning and Systems at Virginia Tech SNAIL Lab, advised by Dr. Bo Ji. My research centers around optimizing machine learning workloads for systems, exploring techniques like efficient self-attention, token dropping, quantization, and many more to make AI more accessible for edge and commodity devices. Prior to starting my PhD, I worked as an ML Engineer at A Fortune 500 Company where I worked on building and deploying their AI-driven recommendation systems for pharmaceutical companies. Beyond research, I actively contribute to the academic community. I served as Secretary for the Virginia Tech CS Graduate Council in 2024-25. Additionally, I served as a reviewer for workshops at ICLR, CVPR and volunteered at AAAI.
